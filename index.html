About Me


Hi, I'm Dani! I'm a software developer and user experience designer. I specialize in creating intuitive and engaging digital experiences, blending technical expertise with a keen eye for design. Whether I'm building seamless applications or crafting user-centric interfaces, I strive to make technology more accessible and enjoyable for everyone.

Alongside my work in software development and UX design, I’m a dedicated freelancer, collaborating with clients to bring their ideas to life through clean code and thoughtful design solutions. Outside of tech, I’m an avid painter, drawer, and knitter—constantly inspired by my love for art and creativity.

Software Development


I have been studying programming languages both in and out of school since 2016, beginning with my interest in HTML and CSS for web development. I gained experience in many areas, creating robust programs, designing responsive websites, and building and maintaining relational databases. I was able to expand my learning outside the classroom through my internships and six month long co-op opportunities, taking the incentive to learn new programming languages (Python, Javascript) and explore related fields (UX design, Data Visualization).
