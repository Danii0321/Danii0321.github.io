I also appreciated the ease of combining Computer Science and Design into a single degree, as well as the variety and intensity of the courses.

I have a lot of passion for what I do, from sketching new logo designs to finding the most efficient algorithm and everything in between. This website was created as a personal project following a semester of instruction, and was built entirely from scratch using HTML and CSS.
