FarmFresh

UX Research, UX/UI Design


This project was the culmination of a semester's worth of work in my Interaction Design course at Northeastern University. We learned the process of designing a web-based product with a hands-on approach, working through stages such as: user research, ideation, prototyping, wireframing, and presenting our findings. We touched on multiple areas of interest in the field of interaction design, balancing the creative process with interviewing users to understand their needs. With this information, we were then able to improve our products and build intuitive and accessible digital interfaces.


What is FarmFresh?

FarmFresh is an ideal resource for those seeking to shop at local, eco-friendly businesses. It helps users discover fresh produce and other goods from small, local vendors instead of large corporations. FarmFresh makes it easy to locate in-season, locally sourced products.




Mobile View

In order to learn more about responsive design, we were tasked with creating a complementary draft of our project in a mobile view. Adapting the design to suit smaller screens while maintaining the aesthetic, functionality, and readability of all the elements was a excellent learning experience.
